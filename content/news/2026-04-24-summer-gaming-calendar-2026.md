# The 2026 Summer Gaming Calendar Is Stacked — Every Major Release From May Through August

April is wrapping up with one of the most extraordinary release windows in memory. And summer isn't letting up. Between confirmed dates, strong release windows, and what the showcase season is expected to add, May through August 2026 is shaping up as the strongest summer gaming period in years. Here's everything confirmed and what's still incoming.

---

## May 2026

**Forza Horizon 6** — May 15 (Premium early access), May 19 (standard)
Xbox Series X|S, PC, Xbox Cloud. Free on Game Pass. Japan setting. Mount Fuji, Tokyo expressways, full seasonal rotation. The racing game of 2026.

**Mixtape** — May 7
PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC. Narrative road trip game from ex-Dontnod developers with a music-driven story structure.

**Indiana Jones and the Great Circle** — May 12 (Switch 2)
Machine Games' acclaimed action-adventure finally lands on Nintendo hardware. Strong reviews at original launch make this a confident Switch 2 pickup.

**007: First Light** — May 28
PS5, Xbox, PC. IO Interactive's James Bond origin story. Lana Del Rey composed and performed the theme. A game from the Hitman studio about the world's most famous spy — this is one of the most anticipated of the year.

**Crashout Crew** — May 28
Xbox Series X|S, Xbox on PC, Cloud, Steam. Physics-based co-op delivery chaos. Day one on Game Pass.

**Lego Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight** — May 29
Multi-platform. Open-world Gotham, multiple Batman eras, and a gameplay scope that sounds like the most ambitious Lego game in years.

---

## June 2026

**Final Fantasy VII Rebirth** — June 3 (Switch 2)
Square Enix's acclaimed second chapter of the FF7 Remake trilogy arrives on Nintendo Switch 2. One of 2024's highest-rated games now portable.

**The Duskbloods** — June window (TBC)
FromSoftware's Switch 2 exclusive. The online multiplayer action game set in a world of demon hunters and divine entities. Potentially the most anticipated Switch 2-exclusive game of the year.

**Xbox Games Showcase** — June 7
Microsoft's summer showcase. Starfield Switch 2 expected. Gears of War: E-Day deep dive expected. Fable update expected. Whatever is coming next from the studios that haven't shown anything yet.

**Big Walk** — June TBC
The next game from House House (Untitled Goose Game). Confirmed for PS Plus inclusion across all tiers at launch. Co-op, proximity chat, puzzle-driven outdoor adventure.

---

## July 2026

**Rhythm Heaven Groove** — July 2
Switch 2. Nintendo's beloved rhythm series returns for the first time in a decade. 60fps, new and classic games.

**Assassin's Creed Black Flag Resynced** — July 9
PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC. Ground-up remake of the 2013 pirate adventure. Ray tracing, reworked missions, Matt Ryan returning as Edward Kenway. $59.99. Day one on Ubisoft+.

**Splatoon Raiders** — July 23
Switch 2 exclusive. Single-player focused Splatoon spinoff with optional 4-player co-op. Treasure hunting across the Spirhalite Islands with Deep Cut.

---

## August 2026

**Metal Gear Solid Master Collection Vol. 2** — August 27
PS5, Xbox Series X|S, Switch 2, Switch, PC. The continuation of Konami's MGS compilation — expected to include MGS3, MGS4, and Peace Walker based on franchise timeline.

**Elden Ring: Nightreign** — August Window
FromSoftware's co-op multiplayer expansion of Elden Ring's universe. One of the most anticipated FromSoftware releases in years, targeting the summer window based on developer comments.

---

## Still Unannounced But Expected

**GTA 6 marketing blitz** — Summer 2026
Take-Two confirmed summer marketing. Trailer 3, gameplay reveal, and pre-order opening are all expected before August.

**Star Fox for Switch 2** — Multiple credible leakers say this month or summer.

**Nintendo Direct** — At minimum one major Direct is expected before fall. Fire Emblem: Fortune's Weave needs a date. The Duskbloods needs a date. Something else is coming.

April's wave is nearly over. Summer is already loaded. This is an exceptional year.
