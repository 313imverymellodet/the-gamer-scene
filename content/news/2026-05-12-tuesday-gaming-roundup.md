May is picking up speed. Two significant releases landed today, one of the year's most anticipated Early Access launches is 48 hours away, and Forza Horizon 6 Premium Early Access opens this Saturday. Here's everything you need to know.

## Indiana Jones and the Great Circle Is Now on Nintendo Switch 2

**Indiana Jones and the Great Circle** arrived on Switch 2 today — marking the first major Xbox-owned game to port specifically to Nintendo's hardware. The title launched on Xbox Series X|S and PC in December 2024, came to PS5 in April 2025, and now arrives on Nintendo's platform with the full game plus **The Order of Giants DLC** included in a complete package.

Machine Games' acclaimed action-adventure puts you in Indy's boots in 1937, taking you from Marshall College to the Vatican, Egypt, and Sukhothai as you piece together a globe-spanning mystery. It was one of 2024's highest-rated games. Nothing has diminished it since.

For Switch 2 owners who've been watching Xbox and PC players rave about this one for over a year: your time is now. The Switch 2 hardware handles it in a way the original Switch never could have managed. If you haven't played it, this is one of the best things on the platform right now.

## Directive 8020 Launches to a Divided but Positive Critical Response

**Directive 8020** has a Metacritic rating of **72** and an OpenCritic score of **76** — the newest entry in Supermassive Games' Dark Pictures Anthology and, by most accounts, the most ambitious one yet.

Set aboard the colony ship **Cassiopeia**, the cinematic sci-fi horror game places players against a shapeshifting alien threat on the distant planet Tau Ceti f. **Lashana Lynch** stars as astronaut Brianna Young. The game features Supermassive's trademark branching narrative with a new **Turning Points** mechanic — you can rewind to earlier story moments and explore alternative paths, making the decision tree more accessible without removing its tension. The Thing-influenced paranoia runs deep: nobody aboard Cassiopeia knows who has been replicated by the alien organism.

> "Directive 8020 is by far the best Dark Pictures game, embracing more stealth gameplay, making the branching story feel seamless, and setting a new bar for the genre." — Multiple reviewers

The critical split is real. Some outlets scored it 8s and 9s. Others hit 5s and 6s, citing weak dialogue and tiresome stealth sections. But even the lower scores acknowledge this as a step forward for the series. Available now on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC. Not on Game Pass.

## Also Launching Today

**Call of the Sea 2** (PS5, Xbox, Switch 2, PC) — The sequel to the 2020 Lovecraftian narrative puzzle adventure. Players journey across the globe uncovering ancient horrors in a game built on atmosphere, mystery, and puzzle design.

**Clockwork Ambrosia** (PC) — A steampunk Metroidvania run-and-gun platformer with deep weapon customisation. Has been quietly building anticipation in the Metroidvania community ahead of launch.

## Coming This Week

**Thursday May 14 — Subnautica 2 Early Access**

One of the most-wishlisted games on Steam enters Early Access on PC and Xbox, day one on Game Pass. The underwater survival sequel from Unknown Worlds includes a substantial ocean to explore, new creatures, the core survival loop, and co-op support at launch. If you're comfortable with Early Access, Thursday is your day. If you want a complete game, wait.

**Saturday May 15 — Forza Horizon 6 Premium Early Access**

Three days. Premium Edition holders get four days of early access before the May 19 standard launch. Japan setting. Over 550 cars. Mount Fuji to Tokyo expressways. PC Gamer has already called the Japan map "the series' best yet." Free on Game Pass from day one on the 19th.
