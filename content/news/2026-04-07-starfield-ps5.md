date: 2026-04-07
---

Bethesda's divisive space RPG Starfield touched down on PlayStation 5 on April 7, marking a landmark moment in the Xbox and PlayStation relationship. The PS5 version takes full advantage of the DualSense controller — adaptive triggers, touchpad support, and the lightbar all contribute to a more immersive experience than what the original Xbox release offered.

PS5 Pro owners can choose between Performance Mode (higher frame rates) or Pro Visual Mode (enhanced graphics quality), giving that user base a reason to revisit what may be the most divisive open-world game Bethesda has ever shipped.

The release also came bundled with the new Free Lanes and Terran Armada DLC, giving PS5 players a more complete version of the game than what launched on Xbox back in 2023. Bethesda has promised further updates that will eventually turn this into the "most complete version" of Starfield ever. Whether that's enough to win over Sony fans who skipped it the first time around is a question the sales charts will answer soon enough.
