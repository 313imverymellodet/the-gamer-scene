As of this week, Rockstar Games has entered the fiscal year of GTA 6's planned launch — an important milestone analysts say signals no further delay is likely. Set for November 19, 2026 on PS5 and Xbox Series X|S, the game has already survived two pushbacks from its original Fall 2025 date.

A Take-Two earnings call next month is expected to provide even more confidence on the timeline. The company has everything riding on this release — it needs to perform — and with Rockstar now inside the release fiscal year, the financial pressure to deliver on time has never been higher.

Expect the marketing blitz to kick into high gear this summer. The second trailer dropped last year to record-breaking numbers, and a third is widely expected around E3 season. Thirteen years after GTA V launched on PS3, we are finally — genuinely — almost there.
