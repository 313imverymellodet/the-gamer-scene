title: "Larian announces next RPG set in 'the age before Faerûn'"
blurb: "Swen Vincke teases a prehistoric fantasy with eight-player co-op. No engine footage yet."
category: Reveal
date: 2026-04-17
---

Larian Studios founder Swen Vincke teased the studio's next project in a brief social media post: a prehistoric fantasy set in the age before the Forgotten Realms existed as we know them. The post confirmed eight-player co-op and a new engine, though no gameplay footage has been shown.
