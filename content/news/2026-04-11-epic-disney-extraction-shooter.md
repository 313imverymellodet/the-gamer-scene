A Bloomberg report that broke last week revealed Epic Games is in development on an all-new extraction shooter built around Disney characters — completely separate from Fortnite. The reported move positions Epic squarely against the likes of Marathon and Arc Raiders in the increasingly crowded extraction shooter space, with the Disney IP presumably bringing a built-in mass-market audience that neither competitor can match.

Details remain thin — no release window, no confirmed character roster, and no official announcement from Epic — but Bloomberg's track record on gaming scoops makes this one worth watching. Fortnite itself has been struggling with declining player counts, which may have accelerated the push for a new flagship title.

Whether Mickey with a shotgun becomes the next big live-service title remains to be seen, but the concept alone has already set the internet ablaze. Disney's recent willingness to license its IP across the gaming space — Fortnite collabs, the upcoming Indiana Jones expansion in other games — suggests this isn't as far-fetched as it sounds. This one is worth keeping an eye on.
