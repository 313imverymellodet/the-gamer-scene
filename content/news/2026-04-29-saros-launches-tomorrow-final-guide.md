## What Deluxe Players Are Saying After 48 Hours

The 48-hour early access community feedback has been extensive and overwhelmingly positive. The key themes emerging:

**The loop is exactly what was promised.** Every reviewer who said the 30-minute run structure creates stronger "one more" momentum than Returnal's longer sessions was right. Players are consistently reporting that they meant to stop and didn't. The balance between dangerous enough to feel meaningful and short enough to feel immediately retryable is landing.

**Rahul Kohli carries the narrative.** Multiple players who went in with modest expectations for the story are posting genuine praise for his performance as Arjun Devraj. The character work is stronger than Returnal's, which is saying something.

**The Parry is deep but learnable.** The new defensive mechanic isn't as intimidating in practice as it sounds on paper. Several players note they went from struggling with it to feeling fluent within three or four hours. It adds a skill ceiling without being a barrier to progression.

**PS5 Pro players are getting a significant visual boost.** Those on base PS5 report no performance issues, but Pro owners are specifically highlighting the visual uplift as substantial — one of the stronger demonstrations of what the Pro hardware enables.

**One honest caveat:** The story is good, not great. The narrative's most consistent criticism from both reviews and early players is that the Carcosa lore doesn't always land its connections clearly enough. It's compelling but occasionally cryptic to the point of confusion rather than intrigue.
