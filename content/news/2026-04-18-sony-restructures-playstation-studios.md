---
title: "Sony restructures PlayStation Studios around three 'pillars'"
blurb: "The reorg collapses twelve studios into Narrative, Service, and Experimental arms. Insiders call it a belated admission."
category: Industry
date: 2026-04-18
---

Sony has announced a major internal restructuring of PlayStation Studios, consolidating its twelve development arms into three strategic pillars: Narrative, Service, and Experimental. The move, confirmed in an internal memo obtained by TGS, represents a significant shift in how the company organizes game development priorities.

Insiders describe the reorg as a "belated admission" that the previous structure — which grew organically through acquisitions — had become unwieldy. The Narrative pillar will house studios focused on single-player experiences, Service will cover live games and ongoing titles, and Experimental will serve as an incubation arm for new concepts.
