Housemarque has confirmed that **Saros** has officially gone gold ahead of its April 30 PS5 launch — meaning the final build is complete and approved for release. No more delays.

The Returnal spiritual successor takes players to **Carcosa**, a lost off-world colony caught under a perpetual eclipse. Players control **Arjun Devraj**, a Soltari Enforcer navigating bullet-hell encounters and a roguelite loop that Housemarque says is intentionally shorter per run than Returnal — targeting 30-minute sessions designed to keep the tension high without the crushing helplessness of marathon attempts. A new **Parry mechanic** adds a skill ceiling that the studio says rewards mastery.

In a piece of deliberate symmetry, April 30 is the exact **five-year anniversary** of Returnal's PS5 launch on April 30, 2021.

**Editions and pricing:**
- Standard Edition: $69.99
- Digital Deluxe Edition: $79.99 — includes 48 hours early access + three exclusive armor sets
- Pre-order bonus (both editions): Hands of Shore Armour Set

PS5 Pro enhancements confirmed. DualSense adaptive triggers and near-instant SSD loading confirmed.

**Saros launches April 30, 2026. PS5 exclusive.**
