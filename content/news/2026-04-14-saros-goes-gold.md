Housemarque confirmed this week that Saros has officially gone gold ahead of its April 30 PS5 launch — meaning the final build is complete and approved for release. The studio's follow-up to Returnal takes players to Carcosa, a lost off-world colony trapped under a perpetual eclipse, with protagonist Arjun Devraj battling through bullet-hell encounters and a roguelite loop that the developer says is intentionally shorter-form than Returnal.

Early access via the Deluxe Edition ($79.99) starts 48 hours before launch, meaning dedicated players can get in on April 28. Standard edition buyers get access on April 30.

In a piece of beautiful symmetry, April 30 is the exact five-year anniversary of Returnal's original release. Housemarque clearly planned the date intentionally — and for a studio that built one of the most beloved PS5 exclusives of its generation, that kind of attention to mythology feels right. Mark the calendar.
