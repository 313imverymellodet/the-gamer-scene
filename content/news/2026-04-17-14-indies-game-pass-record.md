title: "A record 14 indies broke 1M copies on Game Pass this quarter"
blurb: "Microsoft's internal numbers, obtained by TGS, show the curation bet is paying off."
category: Exclusive
date: 2026-04-17

Internal Microsoft data obtained by The Gamer Scene shows that 14 independent games exceeded one million copies played on Game Pass in Q1 2026 — a record for the platform. The figures suggest Microsoft's renewed focus on indie curation is yielding measurable results.
