---
title: "Unity posts first profitable quarter since 2022 layoffs"
blurb: "Matt Bromberg's turnaround appears to be working. The runtime fee fallout still lingers."
category: Industry
date: 2026-04-15
---

Unity Technologies reported its first profitable quarter since the 2022 restructuring that saw nearly 4,000 jobs cut. CEO Matt Bromberg, who took over following the runtime fee controversy, credited renewed developer trust and a refocused enterprise strategy. Community sentiment, however, remains cautious.
