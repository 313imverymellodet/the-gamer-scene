---
title: "FromSoftware's Miyazaki: 'We are done with open worlds — for now'"
blurb: "A rare interview in Famitsu suggests the next project returns to tight, authored spaces."
category: Interview
date: 2026-04-16
---

In a rare in-depth interview with Famitsu, FromSoftware president Hidetaka Miyazaki suggested the studio's next project would return to the tightly authored, interconnected spaces that defined Sekiro and Dark Souls. "The open world served its purpose," Miyazaki said. "We learned what we needed to learn from it."
