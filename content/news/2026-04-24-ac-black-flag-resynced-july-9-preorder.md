# AC Black Flag Resynced Preorders Are Live — July 9 Release, Editions Explained, and the Collector's Edition Has a Kenway Statue

Yesterday's Ubisoft showcase made it official. **Assassin's Creed Black Flag Resynced** is real, it looks extraordinary, and it launches **July 9, 2026** on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC. Preorders are live now across all platforms. Here's every edition, what it includes, and whether the Collector's Edition with the Edward Kenway statue is worth the $199.99.

## Standard Edition — $59.99

The base game. Everything from yesterday's showcase: the ground-up remake in the Anvil Engine, ray tracing, Dolby Atmos, dynamic weather, seamless ship-to-shore transitions, reworked tailing missions, Observe Mode, expanded naval combat, new officers, new story content featuring Matt Ryan returning as Edward Kenway, and the new pet companion.

**Preorder Bonus:** The **Blackbeard Crimson Pack**, which includes a unique pistol with a distinct perk, a unique sword with its own perk, and an Edward costume. These are cosmetic/gameplay additions that won't affect the critical path but add starting flavour.

Day one on **Ubisoft+** for subscribers.

## Deluxe Edition

Includes everything in the Standard Edition plus:

- **Master Assassin Character Pack:** A unique sword, pistol, and trinket each with distinct perks, plus an exclusive costume for Edward
- **Master Assassin Naval Pack:** An exclusive sail set, ship's pet, crew attire, wheel, figurehead, and hull trim for the Jackdaw

The Deluxe Edition is the sweet spot for players who want cosmetic variety without the full Collector's price. The naval pack in particular — customising the Jackdaw is a core part of the Black Flag identity — adds meaningful visual personalisation.

## Collector's Edition — $199.99 / €199.99 / £174.99

The full package for the most dedicated fans:

- Physical copy of the game
- **Edward Kenway statue** — the centrepiece, showing the Captain lounging on a throne in full pirate regalia
- **Faux-leather folio** with expedition notes design
- A **map** of the Caribbean setting
- A **page of sheet music**
- A **steelbook** for the game
- A **skull badge**
- Cosmetic DLC

At $199.99, the Collector's Edition is a luxury item priced accordingly. The Kenway statue will be the key selling point — based on the Amazon France listing that went live early (and was quickly taken down), the craftsmanship looks excellent for the price point. If you're a long-term Assassin's Creed fan who remembers Black Flag as a formative experience, this is the kind of release you'd get the Collector's Edition for.

## What Ubisoft+ Covers

Ubisoft+ Premium subscribers get Resynced on **day one at no additional cost**. If you already have Ubisoft+ and play regularly, you're sorted. If you don't, the Standard Edition at $59.99 is the most straightforward purchase.

## The Bigger Context

Resynced is priced at $59.99 rather than the increasingly common $69.99 or $79.99 premium ceiling. That's a deliberate positioning — this is a remake of a 2013 game, not a new IP, and Ubisoft has priced it to reflect that. It's also worth noting that Ubisoft had a difficult 2025 commercially, and Resynced launching as a confident quality product at an accessible price point is part of what the company needs going into the second half of 2026.

Pre-orders are live now at all major retailers and digital storefronts.

**Assassin's Creed Black Flag Resynced launches July 9, 2026 on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC. Standard: $59.99. Day one on Ubisoft+. Preorders live now.**
