After spending time in Early Access and a full 1.0 launch on PC and Switch, **Hades 2** officially arrived on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S on April 14 — and console players are getting the definitive version.

The PS5 and Xbox builds launch with **Patch 2** already included, meaning every balance fix, story addition, and quality-of-life update from the PC and Switch versions is baked in on day one. Both versions target **120fps** and hit it consistently. The PS5 build supports DualSense haptics, Trophy tracking, and Activity Cards. The Xbox version is a **Play Anywhere** title, syncing saves between console and PC, and is available **day one on Xbox Game Pass**.

If you've been waiting to play Hades 2 for the first time, you're getting the best-balanced, most complete version of the game. RPGSite called it a **10/10**, GameSpew gave it **5/5**, and the community consensus is that it rivals or surpasses the original Hades — no small feat for a sequel to one of the most acclaimed games of the decade.

**One caveat:** there is currently no cross-save support between PS5/Xbox and the PC or Switch versions. Switch and PC players retain cross-save between each other.

**Hades 2 is available now. $24.99 / free on Xbox Game Pass.**
