---
title: "Valve's Steam Deck 2 leaks show an OLED with 8-hour battery"
blurb: "FCC filings spotted last night confirm specs Gabe has been coy about since January."
category: Hardware
date: 2026-04-18
---

New FCC filings have confirmed long-rumored specs for the Steam Deck 2, including an OLED display and an estimated 8-hour battery life — nearly double the original model's real-world performance. Valve has declined to comment, but the filings are detailed enough to leave little doubt about the hardware direction.
