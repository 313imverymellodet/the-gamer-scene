After two delays and 13 years of waiting, we are now officially inside Grand Theft Auto 6's fiscal release window — a milestone analysts say significantly reduces the chance of another delay.

Grand Theft Auto 6 is currently locked in for **November 19, 2026** on PS5 and Xbox Series X|S. The game was originally targeting Fall 2025, was pushed to May 2026, and then delayed again to November. Take-Two has an earnings call next month that is widely expected to either confirm the date or — in the scenario fans are dreading — announce a third delay. Based on fiscal signals, most analysts expect confirmation.

Marketing is expected to ramp up significantly this summer. So far, Rockstar has only released two official trailers for GTA 6. A third trailer and gameplay reveals are anticipated before the end of Q3.

Meanwhile, a Reddit community mapping project this week produced what fans are calling the most detailed fan-made map of GTA 6's Leonida state yet — cross-referenced from four years of leaks and trailer analysis. Multiple cities, motorways, countryside, mountains, and the iconic bay layout are all charted. It's unofficial, but community consensus says it's looking very close to accurate.

**GTA 6 launches November 19, 2026. PC release date has not been announced.**
