# Trepang2 Hits Game Pass Today — The Best Shooter You Never Played Is Now Free

Today's Game Pass addition doesn't have the profile of Saros or the marketing budget of Diablo 4. But for a certain kind of shooter fan — the kind who remembers the original F.E.A.R. fondly and has been waiting for something that recaptures its specific brand of controlled chaos — **Trepang2** joining Xbox Game Pass today is the most personally relevant gaming event of the week.

## What Is Trepang2?

Trepang2 is a **first-person shooter** developed by Team Clout and published by Trepang Studios, originally released on PC in June 2023. It's built around a specific design philosophy: give the player superhuman abilities — bullet-time, invisibility, incredible agility — and then fill the levels with heavily armed enemies who would destroy a normal human, and let the chaos of that meeting produce something spectacular.

The F.E.A.R. comparison is unavoidable and accurate. Like that 2005 classic, Trepang2 centres on the feeling of being impossibly dangerous — the kind of power fantasy where a room of armed soldiers is an opportunity rather than a threat, and the slow-motion bullet-time moments are genuinely cinematic rather than just a gameplay mechanic. Blood, physics, environmental destruction, and the satisfying crunch of enemies meeting their various fates are all executed at a very high level.

The story is deliberately B-movie in its sensibility — a super-soldier with no memory escaping a black-site facility, supernatural elements that escalate as the game progresses — but the game knows exactly what it is and commits fully to the tone.

## Why It Matters Now

Trepang2 launched in 2023 to enthusiastic reception from the shooter community that played it and relative obscurity from everyone else. It didn't have a major publisher. It didn't have a marketing campaign. It arrived in a busy year and was largely discovered through word-of-mouth among people who specifically seek out games in the F.E.A.R. tradition.

Game Pass changes that discovery equation entirely. Every Game Pass subscriber who has any interest in shooters now has zero-cost access to one of the most purely enjoyable FPS games of recent years. The friction to try it is gone.

If you have Game Pass and you like shooters: download Trepang2 today, play the first 30 minutes, and decide from there. The game earns or loses you in that window, and for the right player it absolutely earns you.
