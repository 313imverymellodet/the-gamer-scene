After spending time in Early Access on PC, Hades 2 officially launched on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S on April 14. Supergiant's follow-up to the universally beloved Hades expands the mythology-driven roguelite formula with new protagonist Melinoe, sister to Zagreus, and a whole new pantheon of Olympian relationships to build.

The game is also joining Xbox Game Pass in April alongside Final Fantasy IV Pixel Remaster and Planet Coaster 2, making it one of the best Game Pass months in recent memory. On PS5, the game targets 120fps and hits it consistently — the hand-painted art direction pops on a big screen in a way that PC monitors and the Switch's smaller display couldn't fully capture.

For Xbox players, the value proposition is hard to beat: Hades 2 on Game Pass, with Xbox Play Anywhere support meaning your save syncs between console and PC. Console players who've been watching PC friends rave about it since Early Access now finally get their turn — and based on what we've seen so far, they're in for something special.
