Hollow Season doesn't announce its intentions. You plant seeds. You tend a garden. You watch seasons pass. And somewhere in that quiet rhythm, the game finds a way to reach you that few games ever manage.

Dusk Fox has built something that understands grief not as a mechanic to be solved, but as a texture of daily life. The farm expands, relationships deepen, and the world slowly exhales. It is the best thing I've played this year, and it isn't close.

**Verdict:** Essential. Clear your weekend.
