Opening paragraph — state your thesis directly. Don't hedge. The opinion badge already signals this is a take, so lead with the argument.

## Section Header

Use ## to break up longer pieces into scannable sections.

**Bold** for emphasis on key claims or names.

> Blockquotes work well for pull quotes or for setting up the argument you're about to counter.

Write in first person. This is a bylined opinion piece — "I think," "we saw," "the industry has" are all fine. The blurb on the index page already signals the author and viewpoint.

## The Bottom Line

Close with your strongest statement. What should the reader take away? What does this mean for gaming?
